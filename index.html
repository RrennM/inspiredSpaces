PARTIAL STAGING

Perfect For NON-VACANT Homes

Partial Home Staging is for non-vacant homes. We adjust the arrangement of furnishings, help the client figure out what areas in the home need some attention, and add just the right touches from our inventory to give a fresh, marketable look to every home.

This method is particularly helpful for clients who will be living in their home during the sale process and has the added benefit of being more budget friendly than full staging.

FULL HOME STAGING

Perfect For VACANT Homes

Full Home Staging is for vacant homes. When a potential buyer sees a vacant house, it can be difficult to envision that house as home. Staging a vacant home can dramatically decrease the market time for a house and can even increase the purchase price.

We bring furnishings, accents, and artwork to create a well-designed living space that allows people to not only imagine living there but makes them WANT to live there.
